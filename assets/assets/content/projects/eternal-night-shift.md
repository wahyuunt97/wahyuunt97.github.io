## Project Overview

Eternal Night Shift: The Night Servant is a Macbook game that tells the story of a 'tuyul' (mythical creature) striving for freedom from servitude. Inspired by the creators' own experiences, it reflects a desire to work more peacefully without the pressure of constantly overproducing to achieve prosperity.

### Gameplay
- **Stealth Mechanics**: Navigate through shadows to avoid detection.
- **Narrative Driven**: A reflective journey about freedom and labor.
- **Mac Optimization**: Tailored controls and performance for Macbook architecture.

### Achievements
- **Community Reception**: Received positive feedback from 52 exhibition attendees.
- **Rapid Development**: Completed stable version within 25 working days.
