## Project Overview

Appmu Indonesia is a white-label e-commerce platform that enables businesses to launch their own branded mobile apps in under a month.

### Role & Impact
- **Hybrid Migration**: Migrated core features from Native Android (Kotlin) to React Native.
- **Scalability**: Designed the architecture to support easy reskinning and configuration for over 10 different clients.
