## Project Overview

Spatia! is an innovative iOS and iPadOS application that empowers non-interior designers to analyze and optimize their living spaces. Using advanced RoomPlan and SpriteKit technology, Spatia! enables users to scan and create a digital floor plan of their rooms, helping them arrange furniture for various activities to maximize comfort and functionality.

### Key Features
- **Smart Room Scanning**: Generate digital floor plans instantly using RoomPlan.
- **Furniture Arrangement**: Visualize furniture placement and optimization.
- **Intuitive Analysis**: Get recommendations for maximizing space utility.

### Achievements
- **Top 5 Team**: Selected as one of the top 5 teams from Apple Developer Academy Batam for government showcase.
- **High Stability**: Achieved 0% crash rate on the last 3 updates.
