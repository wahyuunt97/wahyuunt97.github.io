## Project Overview

Cativity helps students become more time-conscious by caring for a digital cat that represents their daily activities. Completing scheduled tasks keeps the cat happy, gamifying the productivity experience.

### Key Features
- **Live Activities**: Track task progress directly from the lock screen.
- **Interactive Cat**: Lottie animations respond to user's productivity status.
- **Smart Reminders**: Context-aware notifications to keep students on track.
