## Project Overview

Pande Putri Go is a comprehensive e-commerce solution for a large supermarket chain in Denpasar. It allows customers to browse products, manage orders, and shop from the comfort of their homes.

### Key Metrics
- **Cross Platform**: Simultaneous iOS and Android release.
- **User Satisfaction**: Achieved a perfect 5.0 rating on the App Store.
